# Prohibited Terms: Tidal (Wellness Focus)

**Context:**
Strict adherence to FDA General Wellness guidelines and avoiding Software as a Medical Device (SaMD) claims unless explicitly following the regulated path.

## Forbidden Terms (Unless SaMD)

| Prohibited Term | Why Forbidden | Approved Alternative |
|:----------------|:--------------|:---------------------|
| Monitor | Implies medical device/SaMD | Track, Observe, Check |
| Diagnose | Implies medical capability | Provide insights, Estimate |
| Detect | Implies medical diagnosis | Identify patterns, Suggest |
| Medical Grade | Implies specific accuracy/standards | High quality, Precise |
| Condition | Implies diagnosed medical issue | Pattern, Range |
| Hypertension | Explicit medical diagnosis | High blood pressure, Elevated readings |
| Treatment | Implies medical intervention | Lifestyle coaching, Guidance |

## Approved Terminology

| Approved Term | Usage Context | Example |
|:--------------|:--------------|:--------|
| General Wellness | Product classification | "A general wellness tool intended to..." |
| Educational | Purpose statement | "For informational and educational purposes only" |
| Out of Range | Status indicator | "Values outside typical wellness range" |
| Peace of Mind | User benefit | "Provides peace of mind about health trends" |
| Health Patterns | Data description | "Helps you understand health patterns" |
