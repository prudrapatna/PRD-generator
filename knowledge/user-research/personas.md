# Target User Personas: Tidal

**Primary Persona: The "Unaware At-Risk" User**

*   **Age:** 45-65 (Impact longevity).
*   **Behavior:**
    *   Health-conscious but not fully proactive about BP.
    *   Wants to age well (biological age sync).
    *   Often lacks obvious symptoms ("hypertension is a silent killer").
*   **Goal:**
    *   Understand their heart health without a medical diagnosis.
    *   Make proactive lifestyle choices (nutrition, activity, sleep).
    *   Know when to see a doctor ("nudge").
*   **Pain Point:**
    *   "I don't know if I'm at risk."
    *   "Cuff is inconvenient/uncomfortable."
    *   "I don't know what to do about my numbers."
*   **Motivation:**
    *   Fear of "silent killer" / hypertension.
    *   Desire for longevity and vitality.
    *   Trust in credible, science-backed insights.

**Secondary Persona: The "Wellness Maximizer"**

*   **Age:** 30-45 (Prevention focused).
*   **Behavior:**
    *   Already tracks sleep/activity.
    *   Seeks to optimize every aspect of health.
*   **Goal:**
    *   Validate lifestyle impact on BP.
    *   Optimize performance/recovery.
*   **Pain Point:**
    *   "Is my workout helping my heart?"
    *   "Is my sleep quality affecting my BP?"
*   **Motivation:**
    *   Data-driven self-improvement.
    *   Preventative health.
