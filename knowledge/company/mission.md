# Company Mission & Vision

**Mission Statement:**
To democratize blood pressure management by making it accessible, understandable, and actionable for everyone, regardless of their medical status.

**Vision:**
A future where individuals are empowered to slow down their biological aging and maintain optimal heart health through proactive lifestyle choices, supported by continuous, passive insights.

**Core Goals:**
1.  **Increase Awareness:** Help users recognize signs suggestive of hypertension so they can seek appropriate screening and diagnosis.
2.  **Enable Lifestyle Change:** Empower users to make the right choices (nutrition, activity, sleep) to keep blood pressure in the optimal range.
3.  **Slow Aging:** Align users with their biological age by managing the "silent killer" of high blood pressure.
