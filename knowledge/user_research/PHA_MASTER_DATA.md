## 2. EXHAUSTIVE FEATURE GLOSSARY & DEFINITIONS
### AI & Coaching
*   **Personalized Answers (Basic):** Ask questions about health/fitness data from your wearable and get AI-powered answers.
*   **Personalized Answers (Advanced):** Includes data from medical records and other health apps.
*   **Personalized Answers (Advanced + Suggestions):** Includes advice on how behaviors impact key health metrics.
*   **Fitness Coach:** AI-powered personalized workout plan that adapts weekly, daily, and during a workout (e.g., if equipment is unavailable).
*   **Sleep Coach:** AI-powered; identifies sleep problems, provides motivational coaching, and creates a personalized plan with daily guidance.
*   **Nutrition Coach:** AI-powered personalized meal plan with recipes; adapts to preferences, dietary restrictions, and fitness/sleep changes.
*   **Women’s Health Coaching:** AI recommendations for fitness, sleep, and nutrition based on menstrual cycle, pregnancy, postpartum, or peri/post menopause.

### Health Detection & Metrics
*   **Hypertension (Screening):** Screens for and alerts to persistent signs of high blood pressure.
*   **Hypertension (Screening + Insights):** Adds tracking of BP variability and impact of daily activities (stress, nutrition, etc.).
*   **Hypertension (Screening + Coaching):** Adds personalized AI-enabled guidance on lifestyle changes.
*   **Diabetes (Risk Assessment & Coaching):** Screens for signs of **Insulin Resistance**; provides AI-enabled coaching to lower risk.
*   **Diabetes (Pre-diabetes Screening & Coaching):** Screens for signs of pre-diabetes; requires physician consultation.
*   **Diabetes (Screening & Coaching):** Screens for signs of diabetes; coaching aligns with medical management plan.
*   **Unusual Trend Detection:** Notifies about unexpected changes in metrics signaling physical strain, stress, or potential illness.
*   **Respiratory Illness Insights:** Specifically alerts to metrics due to respiratory issues and tracks recovery (e.g., cough duration).
*   **Symptom Checker (Advanced):** Connects wearable data and Personal Health Records (PHR) to improve matching accuracy.
*   **Health Record Storage + AI Summary:** Secure storage + AI-powered clear summary of complicated medical data.
*   **Readiness & Recovery (Advanced):** Real-time assessments throughout the day with behavioral insights.
*   **Gait Asymmetry:** Tracks walking/running patterns to assess asymmetry and prevent injuries.

## 6. SEGMENT INSIGHTS: PROACTIVE ADOPTERS
*   **Current Behavior (US):** 31% currently pay for a H&F app (vs 24% gen-pop).
*   **Attitudes:** Highest excitement for AI (68% in US). 79% want a "holistic view."
*   **Exercise (US):** Most common: Walking, Strength Training, Jogging. PAs are significantly more likely to do strength training.
*   **Experience Level:** PAs more likely to identify as "Advanced" in walking, cardio machines, and strength training.
*   **Motivation:** Seeking accountability, structure, and early warning signs ("Sometimes you don’t know there is a problem until you’re in the hospital").

---

## 7. USER ATTITUDES & PAIN POINTS
*   **Holistic View:** ~76-78% of users across all markets want a single view of heart, fitness, sleep, and stress.
*   **Actionable Insights:** ~62-78% want advice on *how to act* on their data, not just the data itself.
*   **AI Enthusiasm:** 60-76% are excited about AI in health, though some hesitancy exists in the UK (61%).
*   **Wearable Necessity:** ~65-70% prefer wearing a device to get full app functionality.

## 8. REGIONAL NUANCES
*   **Japan:** Extremely high value placed on **Sleep Coach** ($2.36 WTP) and **Personalized Answers** ($2.23 WTP).
*   **UK:** High price sensitivity; significant drop-off if price moves from $9.99 to $12.99.
*   **Germany:** Samsung brand is a major competitor; strong interest in **Diabetes Risk Assessment** ($1.51 WTP).
*   **US:** Strongest Apple ecosystem lock-in; Proactive Adopters are the primary growth engine for subscriptions.
