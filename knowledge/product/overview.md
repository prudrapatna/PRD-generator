# Product Overview: Tidal

**Product Concept:**
A passive, non-invasive blood pressure awareness tool designed to identify potential indicators of hypertension and guide users toward proactive lifestyle management.

**Key Features:**
1.  **Passive BP Sensing:** Inferred/estimated blood pressure insights categorized as:
    *   In Range
    *   Slightly Out of Range
    *   Out of Range
2.  **Variability Tracking:** Insights into BP variability relative to personal baseline and absolute BP with calibration.
3.  **Lifestyle Impact:** Correlation analysis showing how nutrition, physical activity, and sleep patterns impact BP levels.
4.  **Actionable Nudges:** Highlight estimates/trends and nudge users when consulting a healthcare expert might be helpful.

**Value Proposition:**
*   **Awareness:** "Am I at risk?" (without explicit medical diagnosis).
*   **Guidance:** "What can I do today?" (lifestyle factors).
*   **Empowerment:** Slow down biological aging through heart health.
