# Simulated User Research Responses

Here are 10 randomized, diverse user personas and their responses to the Blood Pressure Monitoring Features survey.

---

## Respondent 1: The Tech-Savvy Optimizer
*Context: Uses wearables, interested in wellness, high trust in tech.*

**1. Frequency:** Weekly
**2. Methods:** Smartwatch / Wearable device, Home blood pressure cuff
**3. Knowledge:** 4
**4. Concept A (Wellness):** 5 (Extremely likely)
**5. Concept B (Medical):** 3
**6. Preference:** Concept A (Wellness / Coaching focus)
**7. Barriers:** I would prefer to wait and re-measure at home first
**8. Motivators:** If the app explained *why* the reading was unusual, If the app notification was marked as "urgent"
**9. Lifestyle Change:** 5 (Very likely)
**10. Credibility:**
   * FDA: 3
   * Studies: 5
   * Transparency: 5
   * Experts: 2
   * Success Stories: 2
   * Education: 4
**11. Open Feedback:** "I'd love deep integration with Apple Health and specific correlations between my HRV and BP."
